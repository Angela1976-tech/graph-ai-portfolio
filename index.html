About the course

Live Online Certification — Generative AI for Work & Research Productivity

This eight‑week, hands‑on programme demystifies no‑code AI tools so you can automate repetitive tasks, organise knowledge, and accelerate academic or professional output.

  • Automate document, spreadsheet & data workflows
  • Streamline literature reviews & knowledge management
  • Develop custom chatbots for projects & teams
  • Create professional visuals & presentations with AI
  • Apply skills in a capstone project tailored to your field

Real‑time support via workshops, help sessions, and online forums.

Audience: Students, faculty, and researchers in health & life‑science fields — no coding experience required.
